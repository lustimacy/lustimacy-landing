# Lustimacy Marketing — Outreach Library

This folder is the source of truth for outreach copy. Every email, pitch,
tweet, or post you send during the phase-0 sprint should start here.

## What's here

- `outreach/clubs/` — Club partnership outreach emails. 10 DE, 10 NL.
  Personalize the bracketed `[CLUB_NAME]` / `[CITY]` / `[VENUE_DETAIL]`
  before sending.
- `outreach/press/` — 5 press pitch templates. Personalize per outlet.
- `social/tweets-founder.md` — 20 founder-voice tweets ready to queue
  (Typefully / Hypefury / TweetDeck).
- `social/reddit-engagement.md` — Subreddit list + engagement rules.
  No auto-posting. You comment manually as founder.

## How to actually use this

The hardest thing about outreach is *consistency*, not *content*. The
templates exist so you don't have to think about the words — you just
think about the recipient.

### Weekly cadence (suggested)

- **Mon:** Pick 2 clubs from `outreach/clubs/` you haven't contacted. Personalize, send. ~30 min.
- **Wed:** Pick 1 press target from `outreach/press/`. Personalize, send. ~30 min.
- **Fri:** Queue 5 tweets from `social/tweets-founder.md` (rotate so you don't repeat). ~10 min.

That's 70 minutes a week for the entire outreach engine.

### When personalizing club emails

The 3-line opener is the part that matters. Look at the club's website
or Instagram for ~3 minutes before writing the opener. Mention something
specific you noticed (their dress code, their Friday format, their recent
event, a photo). Generic openers go straight to spam.

### When personalizing press pitches

Read 2 recent articles by the journalist. Reference one in your opener.
Each pitch should be a different angle — don't send the same angle to
two outlets in the same niche.

### When queueing tweets

Edit them. The "founder voice" is yours, not mine. Read each one in
your own voice before queueing. If something feels off, rewrite it.
