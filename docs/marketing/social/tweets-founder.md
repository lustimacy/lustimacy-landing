## Notes on cadence

- Don't post 20 in a row. Spread over 4-6 weeks. Mix with replies to others' tweets, retweets of relevant lifestyle / sex-positive accounts, occasional original observations.
- After the first 20, you should have enough engagement signal to know which types work — keep doing those.
- Engage with replies *always*. Twitter rewards conversation. A reply thread is 10× more visible than a tweet alone.
- Don't post during low-engagement hours for European audience (00:00–06:00 CET). Best slots: 09:00, 12:30, 18:00, 21:00 CET.

## Twitter rules for this niche

- **Disclose founder status** in bio (already done).
- **Don't auto-follow / mass-follow.** Twitter penalizes it and your audience will look fake.
- **Don't run paid promotion** for adult-adjacent accounts on Twitter Ads (high risk of suspension; the niche is borderline).
- **Treat replies as content.** Some of your best reach will be replies to bigger lifestyle / poly / ENM accounts.
