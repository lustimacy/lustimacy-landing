## Research checklist for each entry

When you research a club, fill in:

1. **Website URL** — check it's still live, business hours
2. **Instagram** — what kind of crowd shows up (photos)
3. **Format** — couples-only? mixed? what days/nights?
4. **Contact** — usually `info@<domain>` works; some have a contact form
5. **Owner / GM name** if findable on the website (much better cold outreach if you can address by name)
6. **Recent press / events** — gives you a personalization hook in the opener

Then write one of the outreach templates with the personalization filled in.
