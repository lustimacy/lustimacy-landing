At Lustimacy, your privacy is fundamental to everything we do. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, date of birth, email address, and authentication credentials (via Google, Facebook, or Apple Sign-In).

Profile Information: Information you choose to provide on your profile, including photos, gender, sexual orientation, desires, interests, relationship status, body type, meeting preferences, and bio text.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect your approximate location to show you nearby users. You can also choose to search by country instead.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including swipes, matches, and feature usage, to improve our service.

Device Information: We collect device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Match you with compatible users based on your preferences and filters
  • Send you notifications about matches, messages, and app updates
  • Ensure the safety and security of our platform
  • Analyze usage trends to improve the user experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Other Users: Your profile information is visible to other Lustimacy members as part of the service
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services (cloud hosting, analytics) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission, secure cloud infrastructure, and regular security audits. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Correct or update your profile information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. Anonymous, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

7. Age Restriction

Lustimacy is intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a minor has created an account, we will immediately delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us.
